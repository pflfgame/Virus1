How to Play 🦠

🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲🎲
There is a 6x6 square with some 🦠 on it. You start at bottom left and 100 health (HP).
At the beginning of each turn, you move to a grid that is sqrt(5) away. If the grid contains a 🦠, you will deal damage equals to HP/2, then receive a healing of Damage/6, both rounded down.
After your action, the nearest 🦠 will split into adjacent cells, and the original one will NOT die. 🦠 in the same grid will merge after that.
(For example, a 🦠 in the corner with 120 amount will split into 2 adjacent cells with 40 amount, then it is still alive because why not)
If a 🦠 is in your grid, you will receive damage equals to its amount. Your defense is NEGATIVE 10. When your HP is not positive or all 🦠🦠 are cleared, the game ends.
